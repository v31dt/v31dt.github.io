Use this structure for each required physical event reflection. Duplicate this post, rename it, and replace the placeholder text.

## Event context

Name of the event, date, location, duration, and why I attended.

## Speaker or organization

Who organized the event? Who spoke? What is their professional background?

## What I learned

Describe the most important technical or professional insights.

## Link with my studies

Explain how the topic connects to Applied Computer Science, internship work, previous courses, or personal projects.

## Reflection

What was useful? What surprised me? Was anything missing or disappointing?

## Future value

Would I attend again? Would I recommend it to other students? How does it influence my professional direction?

## Proof of attendance

Add a photo, badge, ticket, or other proof here.
