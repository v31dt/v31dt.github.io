Attended BSides Limburg on Friday the 13th in Hasselt, and the two talks I liked the most were the one about insights into LLM hacking from Pedro Paniago and the really creative, though not particularly fairly rewarded in my opinion, hacking of browsers from Robbe Van Roey.

Also talked a lot with new, interesting, and really cool people.

P.S. Bug bounty hunters are indeed the Uber drivers of infosec.
